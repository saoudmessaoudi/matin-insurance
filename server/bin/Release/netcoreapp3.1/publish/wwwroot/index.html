Frequently Asked Questions

We answer your most asked questions.

  • What is the importance of insuring your mortgage?

    Purchasing a home is one the biggest investments you’ll make in your life. It is therefore essential to find mortgage insurance that is adapted to your situation.

  • Is insurance company better for mortgage?

    Yes, mortgage insurance from a bank is more expensive than from an insurance company.

  • What's the importance of critical illness and disability insurance?

    Provides financial assistance following the diagnosis of a critical illness or in the event of a disability.

  • What about disability and income insurance?

    Provides you with a monthly income to compensate for the loss of earnings due to a disability. As a result, you can continue to meet your financial obligations as well as cover expenses related to your physical condition.
